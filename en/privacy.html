Privacy Policy

JUNTO Executive Search respects the privacy and confidentiality of information shared through this site.

This Privacy Policy aims to explain how any personal data is handled, in compliance with the Brazilian General Data Protection Law (Law No. 13,709/2018 - LGPD).

1. Information collection

JUNTO Executive Search does not perform automated collection of personal data through this site.

Any data shared occurs exclusively when the user voluntarily chooses to get in touch through external channels such as email, WhatsApp, or meeting scheduling.

2. Purpose of data use

The information provided is used exclusively for professional contact, responses to requests, and the conduct of institutional and confidential conversations.

We do not use data for mass marketing, distribution lists, or unsolicited communications.

3. Data sharing

JUNTO Executive Search does not share, sell, or transfer personal data to third parties, except when strictly necessary to carry out the communication requested by the data subject or by legal obligation.

4. Storage and security

Shared information is treated as confidential and protected by appropriate security measures, consistent with the sensitive nature of the conversations conducted by JUNTO Executive Search.

5. Data subject rights

The data subject may, at any time, request:

  • confirmation of the existence of data processing;
  • access, correction, or deletion of information;
  • additional clarifications about the use of their data.

Requests can be made by email: contato@juntoexecutivesearch.com.br

6. External links

This site may contain links to external communication or scheduling platforms. JUNTO Executive Search is not responsible for the privacy practices adopted by third parties and recommends reviewing their respective policies.

7. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time to ensure legal and institutional compliance. The current version will always be available on this site.

8. Applicable law

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of the Federative Republic of Brazil.
